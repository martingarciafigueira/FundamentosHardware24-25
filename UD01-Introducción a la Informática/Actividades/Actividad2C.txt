
EJEMPLO: 16 en Octal a Decimal

16(Octal) = 6 * 8^0 + 1 * 8^1 = 6 + 8 = 14 

32 -> 3 * 8^1 + 2 * 8^0 = 24 + 2 = 26
45 -> 4 * 8^1 + 5 * 8^0 = 32 + 5 = 37
127 -> 1 * 8^2 + 2 * 8^1 + 7 * 8^0 = 64 + 16 + 7 = 87
64 -> 6 * 8^1 + 4 * 8^0 = 48 + 4 = 52
72 -> 7 * 8^1 + 2 * 8^0 = 56 + 2 = 58
103 -> 1 * 8^2 + 0 * 8^1 + 3 * 8^0 = 64 + 3 = 67
